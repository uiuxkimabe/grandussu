About Us

Grand Ussu located on Jalan Raya Puncak Km. 79 Kopo Cisarua Bogor, inaugurated by Mr. Regent H. Rahmat Yasin, SE.MM. To meet the demand of the guests, we continue to build our hotel with high standards and private villas with a fresh atmosphere, direct access from the side of the road, and a large parking lot.

Escape to a world of refined elegance and relaxation at Grand Ussu Hotel & Convention. Nestled amidst breathtaking landscapes, our hotel offers a haven of comfort and style that promises an unforgettable getaway.
